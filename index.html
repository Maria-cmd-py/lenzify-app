How to Play Lenzify

  1. Fill in your personal information
  2. Complete the quick language quiz
  3. Read each story sentence with blanks
  4. Select words from the word bank to fill blanks
  5. Earn tokens for correct answers!

Tips:

  • Read the whole sentence before choosing a word
  • Look at the surrounding words for context clues
  • Try to guess before looking at the word bank
  • In timed mode, think quickly but carefully
